Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions. In a pan, brown the ground beef with onion and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti.

Classic Pancakes

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Cook spoonfuls of batter on a hot griddle until golden. Serve with your favorite toppings.
